Donations for Dorian

Welcome PA School Community










Hurricane Dorian devastated many lives in the Bahamas. Now that Hurricane Dorian has finally ended, the PA schools are trying to help. We are trying to make a difference in the recovery efforts. Thousands of people are living in tents and more are homeless. The Red Cross is going in and helping these people. They are setting up tents, giving food, water, and medical supplies. The school is making an effort to raise money for the Red Cross so they can help the people in the Bahamas affected by Hurricane Dorian. The 6C Team students are selling pretzels and collecting donations. Here on this website you can keep track of our success. This is a simple outlet for help. You can also get all of your information on how you can take part in all of the things that we are doing.
